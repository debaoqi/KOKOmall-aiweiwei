Trouble Signing In?

Common Issues and Solutions

Forgot Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  • Click on "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  • Enter your registered email or phone number
  • Check your email/SMS for password reset instructions
  • Follow the link to create a new password

Account Locked?

If your account is locked due to multiple failed login attempts:

  • Wait 15-30 minutes before trying again
  • Ensure you're using the correct credentials
  • Contact support if the issue persists

Can't Receive Verification Code?

If you're not receiving SMS or email verification codes:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Verify your phone number/email is correct
  • Ensure you have good network connectivity
  • Wait a few minutes and request a new code

Third-Party Login Issues?

If you're having trouble logging in with Google or Facebook:

  • Ensure you're logged into your Google/Facebook account
  • Check your browser's pop-up blocker settings
  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Use a different browser or device

Still Need Help?

Contact our support team:

  • Email: support@kokomall.com
  • Phone: Available in app settings
  • Response time: Within 24 hours

🔒 Data Security & Safeguarding

Our Commitment to Data Security

At KOKOmall, we take data security seriously. All your personal information, payment details, and transaction data are protected with industry-standard security measures.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Your personal information is stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Payment Security: Payment information is processed through secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and all access is logged and monitored
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

What We Protect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Payment and financial information
  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • Location data (when provided)
  • Product preferences and browsing history

Your Role in Security

You can help protect your account by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Not sharing your account credentials with others
  • Logging out when using shared devices
  • Keeping your device's operating system and apps updated
  • Reporting any suspicious activity immediately

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide clear information about what data was affected
  • Offer guidance on protective measures
  • Work with authorities and security experts to resolve the issue

Compliance

KOKOmall complies with applicable data protection regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and local privacy laws. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

Contact Us

If you have concerns about data security or wish to report a security issue, please contact us at:

  • Email: security@kokomall.com
  • For urgent security matters, please use our in-app support
